WiZiQ Live Classes can be scheduled for any date and time in your time zone. These classes are private to you and can be launched by you and your course students only. You may use "Class URL" to promote your live class in blogs and websites. Your scheduled class gets automatically published in your event calendar. You can launch your class from there. No sign-up is required to give or attend a class. To schedule WiZiQ live classes for your time zone, make sure that you select your time zone in terms of 'UTC' or 'GMT' e.g. 'GMT+4:30' or 'UTC+7:30' in your Moodle profile. Multi-room subscription users can schedule simultaneous classes with duration up to 300 minutes, for 50 participants and use other features. Against 3 recordings available to a single room user they can record more classes and download recordings too.
